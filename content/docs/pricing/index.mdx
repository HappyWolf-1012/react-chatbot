OpenAssistantGPT offers four pricing plans:

Free Plan: Includes 1 chatbot, 1 crawler, 2 files, 10 messages, and support for $0 per month.

Hobby Plan: Offers 1 chatbot, 1 crawler, 2 files, 100 messages, and support for $3 per month.

Basic Plan: Includes 3 bots, 3 crawlers, 6 files, unlimited messages, and premium support for $8 per month.

Pro Plan: Provides 12 bots, 12 crawlers, 24 files, unlimited messages, and premium support for $30 per month.
